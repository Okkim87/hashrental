Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-21

  1. Introduction

    We use cookies and similar technologies to run and improve our website. This Cookie Policy explains the types of cookies we use, their purposes, and how you can manage them.

  2. What are cookies?

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember preferences and support security, functionality and analytics.

  3. Categories of cookies we use

    • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to operate (navigation, security, session management). These cookies cannot be switched off in our systems.
    • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser, used to maintain session state.
    • Security cookies: Used to detect and mitigate fraud, abuse or security threats.
    • Third-party cookies: Cookies set by third-party services we use (for example form handlers or payment providers). We do not use third-party analytics by default.

  4. Third-party services

    We use third-party service providers (for example Web3Forms and payment processors). These providers may set cookies as required to operate their services. We do not control their cookie practices; please consult their privacy notices for details.

  5. Future analytics

    We currently do not deploy analytics cookies by default. If we add analytics in the future, we will disclose the purpose and, where required by law, obtain consent prior to setting such cookies.

  6. Browser controls and opt-out

    You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functionality. For guidance, consult your browser help documentation.

  7. Changes to this Policy

    We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

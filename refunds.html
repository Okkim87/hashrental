Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-21

Refunds are handled case-by-case. If hardware fails and cannot be repaired or replaced within a reasonable timeframe, Nordhash Oy may offer a prorated refund for unused rental time. Refunds do not cover expected mining profitability or lost mining rewards.

How to request a refund

  1. Contact nordhashoy@gmail.com with your rental details and order reference.
  2. We will investigate and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

This policy is subject to applicable consumer protection laws.
